# Introduction to Quantum Circuit Simulation
*by the Quantum Circuit Simulation Project Team of DOST-ASTI*

This material serves as a comprehensive guide to the quantum circuit model of quantum computation and its simulation. This includes basic quantum computing concepts and well-known quantum algorithms such as superdense coding and quantum teleportation. Readers are expected to have prior knowledge of linear algebra, quantum mechanics, and computer algorithms to ensure a deeper comprehension and engagement with the material.
